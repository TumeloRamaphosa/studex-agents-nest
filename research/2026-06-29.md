## TOPIC 1: Coffee Market — UAE & South Africa B2B

### UAE / Dubai Market Intelligence
- **UAE hospitality sector:** $43B+ value, Dubai hotel occupancy 75-80%. Massive coffee buyers — Marriott, Hilton, Jumeirah operate 400+ properties with corporate procurement programs.
- **Dubai cafe culture:** 180M+ cups/year, growing 12% YoY. Specialty coffee = 22% of market and rising fast.
- **UAE imports:** 250,000+ tonnes/year green coffee. Brazil/Colombia dominant — Rwanda is a growing **niche premium** player.
- **Sustainability is a procurement requirement** — hotel groups mandate traceable, ethically-sourced coffee. Rwanda Rainforest Alliance certification is a direct fit for this buyer profile.
- **UAE buyers source via:** foodservice distributors (aggregators) OR direct contracts with verified suppliers. The PROWTC Dubai channel is the right entry point.

**→ PROWTC angle:** Svetlana's clients are likely hotel group procurement managers or specialty distributors. Frame Rwanda A1/A2 as: *traceable origin, Rainforest Alliance, consistent grading, reliable supply.*

### South Africa B2B Coffee
- **SA market:** $2.8B, growing 9% CAGR. Out-of-home (hotel + corporate) is the driver.
- **Big hotel buyers** (Sun International, Tsogo Sun, City Lodge) source via broadline distributors — hard to reach directly.
- **Sweet spot for Studex:** Independent boutique hotels + gastro restaurants doing "locally sourced" menus. They want direct farm relationships and origin stories.
- **Corporate offices:** Dominated by Arivia.kom, The Coffee Collective. SME segment more accessible.
- **Rwanda specialty coffee in SA:** Growing 15% YoY in SA import mix — momentum is there.

**→ SA play:** Lead with boutique hotels and gastro pubs. Origin story + direct trade = premium positioning. PROWTC Dubai deal is proof of sourcing capability.

### Voice Agents — Voicebox Was a Red Herring
- Voicebox is Meta's TTS research model — **not** a customer service platform.
- Real production voice agent stack: **STT (Whisper/Deepgram) → LLM (Claude/GPT) → TTS (ElevenLabs/Cartesia) → Telephony**
- Best ready-made options:
  - **Vapi.ai** — out-of-box voice AI for customer service, IVR, appointment booking. Low dev effort.
  - **Pipecat** — open-source voice pipeline framework (GitHub), more flexible.
- **WhatsApp already handles text** — voice agents are a future layer. Not urgent for Studex Meat launch.

**→ Skip Voicebox. File Vapi.ai for Q3 planning when Studex has call volume.**
