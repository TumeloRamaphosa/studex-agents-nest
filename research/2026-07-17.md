### Key Findings

- **🚨 CHINA OPENS JULY 20 — 3 DAYS AWAY.**

  - Zero-tariff on all imports from 53 African countries, effective **May 1, 2026**

  - SA: **$16.17/kg** | Belgium: $13.25/kg | Italy: $12.20/kg

  - Premium arabica and single-origin prices hold firm
