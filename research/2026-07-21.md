## ☕ TOPIC 1: AFRICA → CHINA COFFEE MARKET

### Key Findings

**🇨🇳 China opened its market to ALL 53 African countries — effective July 20, 2026 (YESTERDAY)**
- Zero-tariff policy under a unified phytosanitary framework
- China's Africa imports surged 23.5% YoY already
- China coffee market: **$42 billion (2024)**, 240,000+ metric tons consumed — up 150% in recent years
- Rwanda set a national export record: **~$148.6M USD in coffee export earnings (2025)**
- Kenya gaining significantly from zero-tariff access

**☕ Ethiopia is China's dominant African coffee supplier — Rwanda is the gap Studex can fill**
- Ethiopia has 26,000+ coffee locations domestically and $5.7B revenue chains already
- Rwanda has zero-tariff access but is NOT yet dominant in China — **first-mover window is OPEN NOW**
- Ethiopian coffee already has established Chinese distribution; Rwanda does not yet
- **Implication:** China's buyers know Africa broadly but haven't fully discovered Rwanda specialty. This is the gap.

**🔴 Luckin Coffee by the numbers (Q1 2026):**
- **33,596 stores** — largest coffee chain in China by far
- New green bean processing plant in Yunnan Province (trial operation)
- **5-year Brazil deal: 240,000 tons, RMB 10 billion (~$1.4B USD)**
- Second-largest green coffee importer in China (40%+ of national imports)
- Sources from Brazil, Colombia, Ethiopia — **Rwanda NOT in current mix**
- Quality standard: Arabica, 180+ blending formulas
- **Implication:** Luckin is locked into Brazil long-term. A Rwanda supplier could target their specialty/seasonal lines, not their commodity volume.

**⏰ CIIE Shanghai deadline is AUGUST 31, 2026**
- Registration closing soon — this is the only direct B2B touchpoint with Luckin/Tim Hortons buyers
- Studex Rwanda A1 (washed, jasmine/stone fruit) and A2 (natural, blueberry/chocolate) are differentiated SKUs
- FOB prices: USD 4.60–5.50/kg — competitive vs Brazil's commodity pricing

**🇦🇪 UAE — High-end hotel market:**
- World of Coffee Dubai 2026 just happened — 17,000+ visitors, 450 exhibitors
- ROR Coffee Roastery (UAE) creating exclusive custom blends for hotel groups
- Hotels want: consistency, certification (Rainforest Alliance, PROWTC), and provenance story
- UAE buyers prefer: air-freight ready, small-batch specialty, ESG/traceability docs
- Studex Rwanda is PROWTC + Rainforest Alliance certified — **box ticked for UAE luxury hotels**
