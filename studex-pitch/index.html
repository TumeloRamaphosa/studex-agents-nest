How It Works

THE PIPELINE

A fully AI-orchestrated farm-to-door operation — from order receipt to delivery confirmation.

  1. Customer places order at studexmeat.com — Shopify-powered storefront with live inventory
  2. Order is routed to Charlie, the AI orchestrator, which assigns delivery and fulfilment
  3. Product sourced from Moutloe Farm or our supplier network (Silent Valley / Noags Butchery)
  4. Packed and dispatched via Dinkoko or My Courier based on routing zone
  5. Client receives WhatsApp tracking updates at every stage — confirmed, dispatched, delivered
  6. Automated review request sent 24 hours after confirmed delivery
