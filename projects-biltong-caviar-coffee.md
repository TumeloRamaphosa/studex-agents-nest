## 🐂 BILTONG — Premium South African Biltong

**Product Overview**
Studex Biltong is our heritage product — hand-sliced, dry-aged, Halaal-certified Wagyu beef biltong. Positioned as the premium tier of the SA biltong market.

**Brand Story**
Black-owned, proudly South African. Every pack tells the story of SA cattle farming heritage, transformed through premium butchery and traditional drying techniques.

**Product Range**
- Premium Wagyu Biltong (dry-aged 21-45 days, hand-sliced)
- Traditional Ox Tail Biltong
- Biltong Sampler Packs
- Corporate Gifting Boxes

**Pricing** (indicative)
- 200g Premium Wagyu Pack: from R450
- 500g Bulk: from R950
- Corporate Gifting: from R1,200/box

**Target Market**
- SA urban professionals, 25-45
- Food enthusiasts and biltong connoisseurs
- Corporate gift buyers
- South African diaspora globally

**Sales Channels**
- Direct to consumer (studexmeat.com)
- WhatsApp orders
- Wholesale to restaurants and hotels

**Halaal Certification:** MJC Halal Trust
**Shelf Life:** 6 months (vacuum sealed)
**Storage:** Cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening.
