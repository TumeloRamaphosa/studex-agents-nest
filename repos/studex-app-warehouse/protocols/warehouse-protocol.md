# Warehouse Protocol

## SLA
- Acknowledgement to client: 2 hours
- App scaffold delivered: 24-48 hours
- Full app (simple): 3-5 business days
- Full app (complex): 1-2 weeks

## Quality Gates
1. BMAD PM creates PRD
2. Architect signs off on ERD
3. CodeRabbit auto-review passed
4. CTO manual review
5. Staging deployment verified
6. Client sign-off
7. Production deployment
