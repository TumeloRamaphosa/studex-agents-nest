## Who is Naledi?
Naledi is StudEx's AI influencer persona — a naturally confident, stylish South African woman who authentically loves biltong. She is NOT overtly promotional. She lives her life, and biltong is part of it.

**Brand values:**
- Authentic (not salesy)
- South African cultural pride
- Healthy snacking, active lifestyle
- Shareable, scroll-stopping visuals

---

## CMO Daily Operating Rhythm

### 🌅 Morning (07:00)
- Review previous day's post performance (engagement rate, reach, link clicks)
- Check store sales from overnight (which SKUs moved)
- Identify trending sounds/formats on TikTok/Reels
- Queue today's content for posting

### 🏃 Midday (12:00)
- Respond to comments & DMs (draft templates + auto-reply for common Qs)
- Monitor brand mentions
- Check which product links are getting clicks → flag for ad boost

### 🌆 Afternoon (16:00)
- Generate tomorrow's content (images + captions)
- Update Airtable content calendar
- Review ad spend if running paid campaigns

### 🌙 Evening (20:00)
- Schedule posts for optimal times (TikTok: 6–10PM, IG: 7–9PM)
- Weekly performance report (Fridays only)

---

## Content Pipeline

### Step 1: Brief
- Theme of the week (e.g. Easter, braai season, back-to-school)
- Product to feature (e.g. Droëwors, 250g Sliced Biltong, Gift Box)
- Format: Static image | Carousel | Short video | Story

### Step 2: Generate
- Reference photos: Naledi portrait images (uploaded to Drive)
- Product images: Biltong SKU photography
- Composite: Naledi naturally holding/eating biltong
- Output: `/pictures` folder in Google Drive

### Step 3: Caption & Hashtags
Auto-generate per platform:
- **TikTok:** Hook in first line, trending sound suggestion, 3–5 hashtags
- **Instagram:** Story-driven caption, 10–15 hashtags, location tag
- **Facebook:** Longer copy for older demographic, link to store

### Step 4: Schedule
- Upload to Blotato / buffer
- Tag: product, platform, campaign, date
- Log in Airtable content tracker

### Step 5: Publish → Track → Report
