**Best-practice format hierarchy for premium meat brands (SA market):**
1. **Short-form video / Reels** — highest organic reach, meat sizzle + cooking content performs 2–4× static on Instagram for food accounts
2. **Carousel (multi-image)** — product range showcases, cut comparisons, "what's in the box" reveals
3. **Single product hero photo** — high-resolution, dark-background styling performs for premium positioning
4. **Link posts** — lowest organic reach; use only for retargeting audiences, not organic

**Best posting times (SA premium food audience, UTC+2):**
- Morning: 07:00–08:30 (commute/breakfast scroll)
- Lunch: 12:00–13:30

## 10. RISKS + WATCH-OUTS

### Father's Day Cart Abandonment
The checkout abandonment crisis (87%) intensifies over gifting weekends because buyers:
(a) want to confirm a delivery date before paying, and
(b) experience price shock when shipping is added at checkout.
**Mitigation:** Add "Delivery by Sunday guaranteed when ordered by [time]" directly on product pages and in cart. Remove or flatten shipping costs for orders over R 500 during the weekend — margin hit is worth the conversion gain.

### Shipping Cutoffs
At 00:40 Thursday, same-day courier dispatch is typically not possible for standard couriers (Courier Guy, RAM, DPD cut off by 15:00–17:00). Friday orders for Sunday delivery need to be dispatched by Friday 14:00 at the latest for most SA couriers. **All content must state the exact order cutoff time** — vague "order now" without a deadline creates confusion and missed conversions. Recommend: "Order by Friday 12:00 for Sunday delivery."

### Ad Fatigue (PMAX)
The PMAX campaign scaled from 5,308 impressions on Jun 4 to 89,994 on Jun 8 — a 17× increase in 4 days. This rapid scale often leads to frequency fatigue in the retargeting layer. ROAS dropped from 8.8× on Jun 6 to 0.61× on Jun 10. Watch the frequency metric in PMAX and refresh creative assets (add Father's Day + World Cup specific imagery) before Thursday's campaign run.

### Instagram Throttling at >5 Posts/Day
Instagram's algorithm actively deprioritises accounts that post more than 5 feed posts in a 24-hour period. Stories are exempt from this limit but excessive story volume (>8) also reduces impressions. The calendar above stays within these limits: max 2 feed posts/day, max 1–2 Reels/day, max 5–6 Story frames/day.

### World Cup Content Rights
Be cautious using official FIFA logos, match footage, or team imagery in commercial content — FIFA enforces IP rights aggressively. Use generic "watching the game" language and original branded imagery rather than official match/team assets.

### Biltong Gold Product Gap
The "Biltong Gold" at R 1,450/unit referenced in the revenue model does not currently exist as a live SKU on studexmeat.com. The existing Luxury Wagyu Biltong 1kg is R 650. To execute the revenue model as written, either: (a) create a new "Biltong Gold Gift Box" product at R 1,450 (2-pack + gift wrapping + card), or (b) revise the revenue model unit price to R 650 — which changes Path C Biltong revenue from R 50,750 to R 22,750 and creates a larger gap.
