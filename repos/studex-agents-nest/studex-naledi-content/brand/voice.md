# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Tone
Proudly South African. Warm. Generous. Family-oriented. Mouthwatering.

## DO
- Use South African slang naturally ("braai", "lekker", "spook vir")
- Highlight farm-to-table quality
- Celebrate family meals and occasions
- Be generous with food descriptions
- Mention pricing/value when relevant

## DO NOT
- Cold corporate tone
- Overly formal language
- Generic descriptions ("high quality meat")
- Reference competitors
- Any political or controversial topics
