## 🗺️ ACT 3 — The War Room Tour (3:30–5:30)

**[Screen: Full War Room — slow pan]**

> "This is the War Room. Built on React and Three.js — that's the 3D rendering engine underneath.
>
> The globe represents our entire business as a connected network. Each node is a part of the system — a product, an agent, a GitHub repo, a content campaign.
>
> The colors mean different things — blue for agents, gold for the central hub, orange for active tasks. You can click any node to see what's happening with it."

**[Screen: OpsDashboard — KPIs scrolling]**

> "This strip shows live business metrics — orders, content published, system status, next tasks.
>
> We built this because as we scale, we need to see everything at a glance. Not open 15 tabs. One screen."

**[Screen: Intel panel — project tracking]**

> "This is our intelligence layer. OpenWolf — where we track all projects and initiatives. Token holders, content pipelines, new market research.
>
> Everything the agents need to make decisions is here, in one place."

**[Screen: Workflow panel — node editor]**

> "And this is the workflow editor — every business process, visualised as a flowchart. Telegram message comes in, routes to the right agent, the agent executes the right tool, responds. No manual routing."
