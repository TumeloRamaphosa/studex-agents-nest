# Logo Design Brief — Studex Wildlife Coffee
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-07-11 | **Author:** Robusca (COO), Studex Meat

---

## 1. Brand Name

**C@f# N0!R** — stylized as: `C@f# N0!R`

> Pronounced "Caffeine Noir" — French for "dark coffee". The stylization is deliberate: tech-inspired character substitutions (`@` for A, `#` for SH, `0` for O, `!` for I) signal that this is a **tech-forward premium African brand** — not a traditional roastery.

---

## 2. Creative Direction

### Concept
**"Circuit board meets the savanna at night."**

A logo that reads as both **high-technology infrastructure** and **raw African natural energy**. Two aesthetics in tension: the precision of a data node and the warmth of firelight over the bush.

The brand should feel like:
- A premium African coffee company that runs on intelligence
- Tech platform energy (think: Stripe, Vercel, Linear) married to African earth
- Confident, dark-mode-first, precise

### Brand Positioning
- **Premium African B2B coffee** — not artisanal-hipster, not commodity-dark-roast
- **Tech-forward origin story** — Africa as an intelligence hub, not just a sourcing destination
- **Natural energy** — the caffeine is from nature, the data is from precision

---

## 3. Color Palette Direction

| Role | Suggested Direction | Avoid |
|------|-------------------|-------|
| Background | Deep near-black: `#080C0A` | Pure black `#000000` |
| Primary | Forest/charcoal green: `#0F2A1E` | Bright neon greens |
| Accent 1 | Electric lime/chartreuse: `#B8FF00` | Pastel yellows |
| Accent 2 | Warm amber/honey: `#D4A017` | Orange-red |
| Text | Off-white: `#F5F0E8` | Pure white `#FFFFFF` |

**Reference targets:** Stripe's precision, a night safari photograph, the glow of coffee roasting in darkness.

---

## 4. Typography Direction

- **Primary (Display/Logo):** Sharp geometric sans — something with technical character
  - Candidates: `Space Grotesk`, `DM Mono`, `JetBrains Mono`, `Geist`
  - All-caps for brand name, wide letter-spacing
- **Secondary (Body/Labels):** Clean humanist sans — readable, warm
  - Candidates: `Inter`, `Source Sans 3`, `Manrope`
- **Monospace (Data/Badges):** For technical elements (cup scores, grades, coordinates)

---

## 5. Visual Motifs (Directional — Not Prescriptive)

### ✅ Do These
- Circuit trace / node network lines as decorative elements
- Topographic contour lines (referencing altitude/origin)
- Coffee bean abstracted as a geometric / circuit node shape
- Latitude/longitude coordinates as a design element (1,800m ASL)
- Subtle grain/noise texture on dark backgrounds

### ❌ Avoid These
- Sun/bird/landscape cliché African motifs
- Warm brown/coffee-bean illustration style
- Serif/elegant scripts (not a luxury fashion brand)
- Cartoon or playful illustrations

---

## 6. Logo Lockup Direction

Three versions needed:

### A. Full Lockup (Header/Website)
```
[C@f# N0!R logomark]   [C@f# N0!R wordmark]
                         [Wildlife Coffee — subtitle in mono]
```

### B. Logomark Only (Favicon, App Icon, Social Avatar)
- Abstract mark: a geometric node/bean hybrid
- Must read clearly at 16px × 16px
- Single color or two-tone (green + lime accent)

### C. Wordmark Only (Pitch Decks, Business Cards)
- `C@f# N0!R` in geometric sans, all caps
- With or without subtitle
