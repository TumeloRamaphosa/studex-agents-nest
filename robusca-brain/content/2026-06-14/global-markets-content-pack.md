StudEx Global Markets was founded by Tumelo Ramaphosa, a technologist with more than 14 years of experience at the intersection of cloud computing and artificial intelligence. Tumelo is not a newcomer to this space — he has spent over a decade designing and deploying agentic systems, autonomous architectures where AI does not merely respond to commands but initiates, plans, and executes. His most significant achievement to date is the StudEx Super Agents platform — the first cloud sandbox in South Africa where AI agents take control of computers, formulate business strategies, and operate as genuine co-pilots for human teams. Nothing like it exists on the African continent. The platform runs on Polsia NestVM, giving each client company a private, dedicated AI virtual machine — enterprise-grade isolation with superagent capability.

It is from this foundation that Tumelo approached the global stage. And the global stage answered.

### The Four Companies

**NtechLab — The World's #1 AI Facial Recognition Company**

Ranked first by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) — the gold standard for AI benchmarking — NtechLab operates in 26+ countries and processes 1.5 billion face matches per second with a response time of 0.3 seconds. Their technology is deployed by governments, enterprises, and security agencies worldwide. Bringing NtechLab into the StudEx ecosystem places the world's most validated facial recognition AI directly within reach of African enterprises and institutions.

**ART Engineering MDC — Modular Data Centre Architects**

Every AI system needs a home. ART Engineering MDC builds that home — modular, TIER III and TIER IV certified data centres engineered to operate in extremes from -60°C to +60°C. With 50+ facilities deployed globally and a rapid 16-week deployment cycle, ART Engineering represents the physical backbone of the StudEx ecosystem. As demand for AI infrastructure across Africa accelerates, ART Engineering provides the deployable, certified, enterprise-grade facilities to meet it.

**Pharmasyntez — Russia's Leading Pharmaceutical Manufacturer**

Ranked in the Forbes Top 3 in Russia for 2025, Pharmasyntez operates 9 production plants, employs 6,000 staff, and produces over 100 million pharmaceutical packages per year. Their portfolio spans critical therapeutic areas including HIV treatment, oncology, and essential medicines. With a clear mandate to expand into Africa, Pharmasyntez joins the StudEx Global Markets cohort at a moment when access to high-quality, affordable medicine has never been more consequential for the continent.

**Project Phoenix + GRM — AI-Assisted De-Extinction Genetics**

In 2024, Project Phoenix completed the first Northwest Rhino IVF procedure — a landmark in conservation science. The project employs SPonWeb BLUP/GBLUP genetic analysis, the same methodology used in elite livestock breeding programs, now applied to endangered species. This is what AI looks like when it serves not just commerce, but the planet. Project Phoenix represents the StudEx commitment to building a future that is not only prosperous, but alive.

And Project Phoenix demonstrates that Africa's ecological heritage — its wildlife, its biodiversity — can be protected with the same cutting-edge tools that power the global economy.
