# Build Report — 2026-07-03

## What Was Built
**Caviar Landing Page** (`studex-caviar/`)

A luxury B2B landing page for Russian caviar targeting the South African hospitality market. Dark navy + gold aesthetic, full product catalogue, trade pricing table, and B2B inquiry form.

## Live URL
**https://gdj07natti0i.space.minimax.io**

## What It Does
- **Hero section** — full-bleed dark hero with blur backdrop, headline, dual CTAs
- **Trust bar** — 4 credibility pillars (Caspian origin, cold-chain, SA customs, B2B only)
- **Product collection** — 4 caviar SKUs (Oscietra Imperial R4,200/250g · Sevruga Premium R3,400 · Siberian Reserve R2,600 · Hackback Royal R5,800), each with image, description, MOQ, and quote link
- **Sourcing section** — direct-from-Caspian story, CITES cert, cold-chain logistics, SA health certs
- **Trade pricing table** — MOQ and per-unit pricing for 250g / 500g / 1kg tiers
- **B2B inquiry form** — name, company, email, phone, enquiry type, volume, message; shows confirmation on submit

## Technical Details
- **Stack:** React + Vite + TypeScript + TailwindCSS
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (headings) + Inter (body)
- **Colors:** #0a0a0f (bg), #c9a84c (gold accent), #f5f0e8 (text)
- **No backend** — form is client-side only (no submissions stored)
