## What It Does

The page is a dedicated China-first B2B export sales asset for Studex Meat's Rwanda coffee division:

- **Hero** — Prominent "China Market Open · July 20, 2026" announcement with animated pulse indicator and red/China colour scheme
- **Why China Now** — Three-panel section explaining zero tariff access, CIIE event details, and demand from Luckin/Tim Hortons China (bilingual EN/CN)
- **Product Cards** — Rwanda A1 Washed (USD 4.80/kg, 12 MT stock) and A2 Natural (USD 5.20/kg, 6 MT stock) with FOB pricing, certifications, cup profiles
- **Landed Cost Calculator** — Shows FOB + freight to Shanghai ≈ USD 5.70/kg (all-in)
- **Buyer Types** — Targets roasteries, hotel groups, importers/distributors, and e-commerce/retail
- **CIIE Roadmap** — Timeline from Aug 2026 (booth confirmation) through Dec 2026 (first container landed in China)
- **Bilingual Inquiry Form** — Fields include: Name, Company, Role, Email, **WeChat ID**, Monthly Volume, Grade Interest, City/Province, Message
- **Nav link added to main coffee site** at `/ciie2026` — accessible from main studex-coffee site nav

**Route structure:**
- `/` — Main wildlife/SA/UAE coffee site
- `/b2b` — SA B2B one-pager
- `/ciie2026` — **NEW** China export page
